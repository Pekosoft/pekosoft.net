<h1>2.1
09-02-2015
</h1>

Fixed a bad bug. 261.626 HZ was C-1 instead of C 0. Because A was wrongly used as chamber-note instead of C, only C, C# and B showed the right octave. Changed Buttons grid to have 12 instead of 14 columns. This places 120, instead of 130, right below 60, and so forth. There are now four rows instead of three, and the highest value / button is 335 instead of 315.
